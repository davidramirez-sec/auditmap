About AuditMap
IT CONTROL FRAMEWORK INTELLIGENCE TOOL

AuditMap was built to solve a problem I kept running into: cross-referencing NIST SP 800-53, ISO/IEC 27001, and SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria manually is slow and error-prone — especially during audit scoping.

This tool maps 50 controls across all three frameworks simultaneously, scores your gap coverage based on existing compliance, and generates exportable evidence checklists ready for audit workpapers.

Built for auditors, compliance analysts, and IT security teams preparing for SOC 1 / SOC 2 engagements.
